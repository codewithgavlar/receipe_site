Chocolate Cake

"Here is a fast and easy cake reciepe to make a chocolate cake"

Diffculty Prep Time Cooking Time Total Time
Easy 10 Min 25 Mins 35 Mins

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter 50g

Eggs x 4

Flour: 200g

Instructions

1

Melt Chocolate and butter together

2

Mix Sugar, flour and eggs together

3

Put into oven and bake for 20 mins at 200c
